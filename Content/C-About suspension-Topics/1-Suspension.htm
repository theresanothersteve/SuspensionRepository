Suspension

Suspension does two things:

  1. It makes the ride more comfortable for the rider.

  2. It keeps the loaded tyres in contact with the road surface.

That's it.

Modern motorcycle suspension has three things that can be adjusted to help do its job:

  1. Spring preload.

  2. Rebound damping.

  3. Compression damping.

Some motorcycles also allow geometry changes that are outside of this guide.
